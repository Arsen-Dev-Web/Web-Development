About Dogs

Dogs are loyal, intelligent, and affectionate domesticated mammals, widely considered "man's best friend" and ideal household pets. As descendants of wolves, they have lived alongside humans for thousands of years, serving as loyal companions, guardians, and workers. Known for their keen sense of smell and hearing, dogs are used in security, search and rescue, and therapy. They are highly emotional, showing love, joy, and comfort to their owners, which helps reduce human stress. With various breeds, they require basic care like walking and grooming, offering unconditional loyalty in return.Dogs are omnivores that thrive on a balanced diet of high-quality commercial dog food (kibble or wet), supplemented with safe human foods like lean cooked meats (chicken, beef), fish, vegetables (carrots, green beans), and fruits (apples, bananas). They need nutrients like protein, carbohydrates, vitamins, and healthy fats.
